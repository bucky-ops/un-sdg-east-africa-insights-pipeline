# Contributor Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We are committed to making this project a welcoming space for collaboration, especially for UN staff, government partners, NGOs, and the open data community.

## Our Standards
- Be respectful and inclusive in all interactions.
- Provide clear, constructive, and professional feedback.
- Focus on policies and impact; keep technical discussions transparent.

## Enforcement
Instances of harassment or unacceptable behavior will be addressed promptly by maintainers.

Thank you for helping make this project a positive, productive environment.
